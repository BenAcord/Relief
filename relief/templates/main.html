Hello!

Welcome to Relief, the app that helps summarize your threat intelligence analysis into a well-crafted presentation. Relief presents a structured approach to recording the data points surrounding a threat event. Each campaign has an associated and layered Cyber Kill Chain, business impact, and courses of action taken. All data can be saved to a database or exported as a presentation based upon a custom template.

If you are new or giving Relief a test run start with the New Campaign menu item to the left.

List Campaigns will display a table view of all your previous campaigns. Edit and export to presentation options exist for previous campaigns.

Stats shows a heatmap of your analysis and defensive capabilities. Use this to highlight tool investment value and reveal areas for improvement.
